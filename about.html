WHO WE ARE

PFALCON REALTY is a forward-thinking real estate firm dedicated to delivering innovative property solutions in Southeast Michigan.

We believe real estate is more than transactions — it's about helping people find the right space to build their lives and businesses. Our team brings local market expertise, strategic thinking, and an unwavering commitment to client success to every engagement.

Based in Royal Oak, MI, we serve buyers, sellers, and investors across the greater Detroit metropolitan area with professionalism, transparency, and results.
