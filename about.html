About PFALCON

The Firm.

Est. 2014
Royal Oak, Michigan
Oakland County Market

Southeast Michigan is not a secondary market.
We've known that since the day we opened.

PFALCON REALTY was founded in Royal Oak in 2014 with a clear premise: Oakland County and the communities surrounding it represent one of the most underappreciated real estate markets in the Midwest. Strong employment anchors, diverse housing stock, consistent demand, and a suburban fabric that continues to attract buyers and investors who know what they're looking at.

We built our practice around that conviction. Over the past decade, we've served buyers, sellers, and investors across Southeast Michigan — not as a regional outpost of a national brand, but as a firm that is genuinely of this market.

Our Market Thesis

Oakland County
is undervalued
by those who
aren't watching.

The Southeast Michigan market is structurally sound in ways that don't always make headlines. Major employment anchors — automotive, healthcare, technology, and professional services — create sustained demand across price bands. The region's housing stock is diverse enough to support first-time buyers, move-up buyers, and institutional investors simultaneously.

Royal Oak and its surrounding communities sit at the intersection of urban amenity and suburban scale. That positioning is rare, and it continues to attract a buyer profile that is increasingly sophisticated — people who run the numbers, weigh commute patterns, and think about resale before they close.

We speak that language. Every recommendation we make is grounded in the same analytical framework we'd apply to our own capital.

How We Work

The PFALCON
approach.

Real estate transactions involve significant capital, compressed timelines, and asymmetric information. Our job is to close that information gap for our clients — through rigorous preparation, honest analysis, and disciplined execution at every stage of the process.

01
Market
Intelligence

We track inventory, pricing trends, days-on-market, and absorption rates continuously — not just when a client engagement begins. That preparation lets us move with conviction when the right opportunity surfaces.

02
Analytical
Rigor

Every transaction gets underwritten. For buyers, that means comp analysis, condition assessment, and a clear view of value. For sellers, it means pricing strategy grounded in data — not optimism.

03
Disciplined
Negotiation

We negotiate from preparation, not improvisation. Knowing a market deeply — its pace, its pressure points, its typical concession patterns — is the foundation of effective advocacy at the table.

04
Seamless
Execution

From accepted offer to closing table, we manage the process with precision. Timelines, contingencies, title, inspections — every detail is tracked so nothing delays or derails the transaction.
